Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions to help you feel more prepared.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person therapy?

+

Yes, numerous studies have shown that online therapy is just as effective as traditional in-person therapy for a wide range of issues, including anxiety, depression, and stress. The most important factor for success is the quality of the therapeutic relationship, which can be strongly established online.

How do you ensure my privacy and confidentiality?

+

Your privacy is our highest priority. We use secure, end-to-end encrypted video conferencing platforms that adhere to international privacy standards (like HIPAA). Everything you discuss with your therapist is strictly confidential, bound by the same ethical and legal codes as in-person therapy.

What technology do I need for a session?

+

All you need is a stable internet connection and a private space where you won't be disturbed. You can use a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a webcam and microphone. No special software is required; you'll simply click a secure link that we send you before your session.

How much does a session cost and how do I pay?

+

Our standard 50-minute session rates are listed on our booking page. We offer slight discounts for booking packages of multiple sessions. We accept payments through major Nepali gateways like eSewa and Khalti, as well as bank transfers and major credit cards for international clients.
