Our Therapeutic Services

A compassionate, evidence-based approach to support your mental and emotional well-being. Explore how we can help you on your journey.

Individual Counseling

A confidential, one-on-one partnership designed to help you navigate life's challenges, understand yourself more deeply, and work towards your personal goals. It is a dedicated space for you to be heard without judgment.

This is for you if you're dealing with:

Personal growth, life transitions, self-esteem issues, anxiety, depression, or stress.

Our Approach:

We utilize a blend of therapeutic modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), person-centered therapy, and mindfulness techniques, tailored to your specific needs.

Couples & Relationship Therapy

A supportive space for partners to navigate challenges, improve communication patterns, and strengthen their emotional bond. We help you build a healthier, more fulfilling relationship together.

This is for you if you're dealing with:

Communication breakdowns, conflict resolution, intimacy issues, pre-marital counseling, or recovering from infidelity.

Our Approach:

We draw from Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and the Gottman Method to help you understand the root causes of conflict and develop new, positive interaction cycles.

← Learn More & Book Session

Anxiety & Stress Management

Learn practical, evidence-based strategies to manage overwhelming worry, workplace stress, and the physical symptoms of anxiety. Our goal is to empower you with tools to regain a sense of calm and control.

This is for you if you're dealing with:

Generalized anxiety, social anxiety, panic attacks, phobias, academic or professional burnout.

Our Approach:

We focus on psychoeducation and skill-building, using techniques from Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and relaxation training to help you manage your symptoms effectively.

Learn More & Book Session →

Support for the Nepali Diaspora

Connect with a therapist who understands your roots. We provide a unique space for Nepalis living abroad to explore challenges related to identity, acculturation, family expectations, and loneliness.

This is for you if you're dealing with:

Cultural adjustment issues, homesickness, intergenerational conflict, and the pressure of balancing two cultures.

Our Approach:

Our care is culturally-informed, providing a bridge between your life abroad and your cultural identity. We offer a non-judgmental space to navigate your unique experience.
